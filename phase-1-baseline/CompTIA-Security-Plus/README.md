## About

CompTIA Security+ (SY0-701) is the baseline DoD 8140/8570 certification required for all privileged access roles in the Department of Defense. It is vendor-neutral, globally recognized, and the mandatory first step before any DoD cyber role. Passing Security+ satisfies IAT Level II and IAM Level II requirements under DoD 8140.
